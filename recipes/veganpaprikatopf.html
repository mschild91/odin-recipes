Vegan-Paprikatopf

A vegan "Paprikatopf"

A vegan "Paprikatopf" is the perfect dish. You are getting a fast and delicious Meal. If you have not much time to cook but you want to eat healthy, this is what you are looking for.

It consists of Rice as a main Carbohydrate Source. It has Peppers and Tomatoes in it. For Meat itÂ´s some vegan minced meat from a Brand called "Beyond Meat". At first you boil the Rice. After that you fry the minced meat with some cut onions. If the minced meat is finished you put some Peppers and Tomatoes in the pan. To put some size and juiciness to the Meal, you fill up the pan with sieved Tomatoes. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes and spice it up. Now you pair it together with the Rice. Finished is your perfect Meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g of Rice
  • 1 can of sieved Tomatoes
  • 2 cans of chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 500g of vegan minced Meat
  • 3 red Peppers
  • Spices (put in there what you like)

Steps

  1. Boil the Rice.
  2. Slice the Onion.
  3. Put it in a Pan together with the vegan minced Meat.
  4. Fry the Onion and the minced Meat.
  5. Put the red Peppers in the pan and let it fry.
  6. Put the chopped and sieved Tomatoes in the pan and let everything cook for 3 minutes.
  7. Spice it up with salt and pepper.
  8. Let everything cook again for 3 minutes.
